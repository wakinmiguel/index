Our Starbucks Favorites


Coffee & Brewed Beverages: Starbucks is known for its freshly brewed coffee, ranging from the classic Pike Place Roast to seasonal blends that highlight unique flavors from around the world.


Espresso Beverages: From creamy lattes and cappuccinos to bold Americanos and macchiatos, Starbucks offers handcrafted espresso drinks that can be customized with different syrups and milk options.


Frappuccino® Blended Beverages: A customer favorite, the Frappuccino comes in a wide range of flavors such as Mocha, Caramel, Java Chip, and seasonal limited editions like Pumpkin Spice or Peppermint Mocha.


Teas & Refreshers: Starbucks also serves Teavana® teas, including green tea, chai tea, and fruit-based iced teas. Their Refreshers are light, fruity, and energizing drinks, perfect for a tropical climate like the Philippines.


Bakery & Food: Starbucks pairs drinks with delicious pastries, sandwiches, cakes, and protein boxes. Popular items include their signature cheesecake, croissants, and breakfast sandwiches.
